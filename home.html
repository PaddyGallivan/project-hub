📖 How to Use the Network
💬
Talking to Thunders
Open any Thunder's URL and use the /ask endpoint — or visit the URL directly for a chat UI. Tell it what you need. It handles it or escalates.

Example:
POST teddy.pgallivan.workers.dev/ask
{"message": "Check KBT events this week"}

Teddy routes it to Thunder-KBT automatically.
🚀
Sending a Thunder Out
"Sending a Thunder out" means asking it to actively do something — check URLs, ping workers, generate reports.

Thunder-Watch: /check pings all your live sites
Thunder-Dev: /check pings all 13 workers
Thunder-Dev: /report full network health
Thunder-Inbox: /urgent shows what needs attention
Thunder-Revenue: /report monthly summary
